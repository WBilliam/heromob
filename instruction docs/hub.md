in the hub menu player can enter one of the following tabs:
    1. stages: a menu with all the battle stages. player can choose a stage and enter the fight. each stage rewards the player with gold/items based on the stage difficulty.
    2. army: a menu displayin all the available creatures the player can use in the army. the owned creatures are fully visible and the not owned yet are in a darker shade. in the army menu the player can purchase new creatures and upgrade the already owned. there are also sorting tabs sorting the creature list based on the element/type/stats etc
    3. buildings: a menu similar to "army" where the player can see the owned buildings, purchase new ones and upgrade already owned. there is no sorting though
    4. items: player can purchase new items, salvage owned items for gold, or fuse owned items to create stronger ones.
    5. spells: a menu similarly to buildings can see the owned spells, purchase new ones and upgrade already owned.
