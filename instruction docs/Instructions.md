## menu
    - units: purchase new units, upgrade already owned
    - building: purchase new buildings, upgrade already owned
    - stages: fight against computer
    - magic: purchase new spells, upgrade already owned
    - gear: purchase new items, upgrade already owned, salvage etc

## stages
    - worlds leading to a boss

## magic
    - types of spells
        - damaging
        - supporting
    - 1 spell can be usable per fight
    - spell has timer that begins loading on the begining of the fight and each time the spell is used, the timer resets

## gear
    - loot from stages
    - types of items
        - weapons
        - armor
        - trinkets
    - each creature can have 1 piece of item (weapon/armor or trinket), but heroes can have 1 of each
